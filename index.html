Take a comic character on a date.

Maybe invite them into your bed after.

Tinder infamously introduced "swipe right" approval or "swipe left" dismissal to the dating scene. Connecting with a new comic book could be just as easy.

Framing comic panels into a Tinder profile invites readers to have their own meet-cute with characters from Drawn and Quarterly's collection. Showing them on social media channels breathes new interest and contemporary intrigue into the world of comics and of Drawn and Quarterly.
